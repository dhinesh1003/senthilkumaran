Our Story

A Family Legacy of Honest, Chemical‑Free Oil

Senthil Kumaran Oil Mill began as a small neighbourhood chekku in Simmakkal, Madurai, driven by one belief: oil should be pressed slowly, cold, and without shortcuts. Today we still use the traditional wooden Chekku extraction method — no heat, no solvents, no chemicals — just seed, wood, and patience.

Our Mission is to bring back the purity of traditional oil to every Tamil Nadu kitchen. Our Vision is to be the most trusted name in wood-pressed oils across South India, one honest bottle at a time.

How It's Made

Our Oil Extraction Process

1
Seed Selection

Hand-picked, high-quality seeds sourced from trusted farms.

2
Cleaning

Seeds are sorted and thoroughly cleaned to remove impurities.

3
Wood Press Extraction

Slow-rotating wooden Chekku crushes seeds without generating heat.

4
Filtering

Natural settling and filtering removes sediment, keeping oil pure.

5
Packing

Bottled fresh in food-grade containers, sealed for freshness.

Delivery
